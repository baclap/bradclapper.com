About

Brad Clapper is a filmmaker living in Los Angeles. Brad studied film at the Ohio State University and moved West to pursue opportunities in the entertainment industry where he has worked in various capacities for a decade. He has a strong background in post production, particularly in visual effects, compositing, and motion graphics. He is a virtual reality enthusiastic and perpetual tinkerer with a passion for creativity and desire to always keep learning.
